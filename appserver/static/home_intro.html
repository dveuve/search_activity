Search Activity

The Search Activity app is a community supported application for understanding Splunk usage and adoption within an organization. There are a few primary use cases that will run throughout the views provided for this app. They are:

  1. Understanding Your Biggest Users - Who are the most frequent searchers or the heaviest consumers of resources
  2. Understanding Any User - Pick an user and understand what types of searches they run, do they share or export search results, and more.
  3. Understanding Usage within the Organization - While not explicitly required, this app will benefit most when integrated with LDAP information so that you can understand usage in the context of the organization. Where do your users lie, and how do they impact the organization?
  4. Tracking New and Important Users - From sending them resources when they first engage, to tagging key user communities so that you can understand the adoption of a specific cohort within the organization, it is important to support your newest and most critical user communities.

Overall, this app should provide you a great amount of context and visibility around your user community. If you are an advanced user, you can also benefit by asking your own novel questions to the dataset. There are nearly infinite different possible queries and interesting avenues you could explore, so take one of the existing searches, reference the help page for a description of the available data points, and start going. If you're not an advanced user, or if you're unable to answer the questions you're looking for, please don't hesitate to ask for help via Splunk Answers, which will be frequently monitored.

Please explore below, and let us know if you have any questions. Thank you!
